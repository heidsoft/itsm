# Product Roadmap Manager

This skill helps manage the ITSM product roadmap, prioritize features, plan releases, and track development progress against business objectives.

### High Risk
1. **Workflow Engine Complexity** - May exceed Q2 timeline
   - Mitigation: Simplify initial version, add complexity incrementally

2. **AI Feature Dependencies** - Requires external AI services
   - Mitigation: Build fallback mechanisms, use open-source alternatives

### Medium Risk
3. **Mobile Development** - Limited mobile expertise in team
   - Mitigation: Consider hybrid approach, partner with mobile specialists

4. **Performance at Scale** - Uncertain performance with large datasets
   - Mitigation: Early performance testing, implement caching strategies

### Low Risk
5. **Integration APIs** - Standard REST API development
6. **Reporting Features** - Well-understood requirements
